Help & FAQ

Q: Is AI replacing doctors?

A: No. VAIDYA's AI provides suggestions to speed triage — all prescriptions must be verified by a licensed doctor before being finalised.

Q: How do I become a verified doctor?

A: Contact the admin with your medical credentials. The admin reviews and marks verified doctors in the system.

Q: Can I edit my profile picture?

A: Yes — after logging in, go to Profile → Edit and upload a new image.

Q: Where are my prescriptions stored?

A: Prescriptions and consultation records are stored in your secure account under 'My prescriptions'.
